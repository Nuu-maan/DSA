Q2: Given two integers L and R, count the number of prime numbers in the range [L, R] (1 <= L <= R <= 10^12).
Source: LeetCode Hard / GeeksforGeeks - Segmented Sieve

Given two integers L and R, return the number of prime numbers in the inclusive range [L, R].

Sample Input:
L = 10
R = 20

Sample Output:
4

Explanation:
The primes in [10, 20] are 11, 13, 17, 19.
